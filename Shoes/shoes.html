Sneakers

An important feature to look for in shoes to be worn while standing is lightness. Especially in daily sports shoes, the lightness of the material provides comfort to the worker and generally does not tire the user's feet. A size 43 men's sneaker shoe is light enough to be 250-300gr. For a size 37 women's sneaker, 200-250gr weight is recommended. However, do not forget that the main task of shoes is to provide support to your feet, which carry the body weight. Therefore, the sole must be strong, which is a feature that is not easily found in very light shoes.
